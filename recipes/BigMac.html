BIG MAC

The Big Mac is a hamburger consisting of two 1.6 oz (45.4 g) beef patties, Iceberg lettuce, American cheese, pickles, chopped onions and special Mac Sauce (a Thousand Island dressing variant, though the recipe remains a secret) served on a three part sesame seed bun.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Burger Patties
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Seeds
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickle
  • Onions
  • American Cheese

Steps

  1. Add sauce to the buns
  2. Put in pattie no1
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add onions, lettuce, tomatoes
  5. Add sauce
  6. Add bun no2
  7. Add pattie no2
  8. More onions, lettuce and tomatoes
  9. More Sauce
  10. Add final bun
